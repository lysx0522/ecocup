About EcoCup

EcoCup is a reusable, biodegradable beverage cup made from bamboo fiber and plant-based plastic. Designed for both hot and cold drinks, it's dishwasher-safe, durable, and lightweight. EcoCup helps reduce single-use waste while offering convenience and style.

Perfect for eco-conscious consumers, students, office workers, and anyone who loves coffee, tea, or smoothies on the go. With EcoCup, you make a smart choice for both your routine and the environment.

Why EcoCup?

  • Made from sustainable and renewable materials
  • Biodegradable after long-term use
  • Elegant minimalist Nordic design
  • Safe for dishwashers and daily use
  • Ideal for hot and cold drinks

Our Mission

We aim to reduce single-use plastic waste by offering a stylish and practical alternative. Our mission is to promote daily sustainable habits through products that blend form and function. With EcoCup, you can enjoy your favorite drink and protect the planet at the same time.
